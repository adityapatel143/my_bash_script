The test command can perform various numeric comparison using the following operators:

Operator	Syntax			Description

eq		INT1 -eq INT2		INTEGER1 is equal to INTEGER2

ge		INT1 -ge INT2		INTEGER1 is greater than or equal to INTEGER2

gt		INT1 -gt INT2		INTEGER1 is greater than INTEGER2
	
le		INT1 -le INT2		INTEGER1 is less than or equal to INTEGER2

lt		INT1 -lt INT2		INTEGER1 is less than INTEGER2

ne		INT1 -ne INT2		INTEGER1 is not equal to INTEGER2
